Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our printing service

Which file types do you accept?

We accept all common file formats including PDF, DOC, DOCX, PPT, PPTX, XLS, XLSX, JPG, PNG, and TIFF. If you have a special file format, please contact us and we'll do our best to accommodate.

How much is delivery?

Standard delivery within Nicosia city is €1.50, while nearby zones are €2.50. We also offer express same-day delivery for an additional €3.00. Delivery fees are calculated automatically when you place your order.

Is my document information secure?

Absolutely. We use secure servers with encryption to protect your files. All documents are automatically deleted from our systems after printing and delivery is complete. We never share or store your files beyond what's necessary to fulfill your order.

What's your turnaround time?

Most orders are printed and delivered within 24-48 hours. For urgent needs, we offer same-day express delivery when ordered before 12pm. Large volume orders may take slightly longer - we'll notify you if this is the case.
